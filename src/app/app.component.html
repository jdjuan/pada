P.A.D.A.

Purpose | Agenda | Documents | Actions

A template for better meeting invites

1.Purpose

Outcome of the meeting

Every meeting should have a well-defined goal, otherwise, you will end up with wandering conversations.

2.Agenda

Points to be addressed

A set of points makes meetings more organized, allowing you to know where you are at, what's the next topic, and how much is left to be discussed.

3.Documents

Reading material

Meetings are more productive when aimed at discussions, not presentations. Send PowerPoint, Word, or Excel files in the meeting invite, and use the allocated time to discuss them.

4.Actions

Relevant tasks

Meetings are more productive when participants take an active role. For example, ask them to bring suggestions to the matter that will be discussed.
