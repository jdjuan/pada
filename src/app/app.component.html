P.A.D.A.

Purpose | Agenda | Documents | Actions

A template for better meeting invites

1.Purpose

What is the expected outcome?

Provide a succinct goal that outlines the expectations of the meeting.


✅ Keeps participants aligned

✅ Avoids irrelevant discussions

2.Agenda

How should the time be allocated?

List the steps required to achieve the goal of the meeting.


✅ Helps participants understand the meeting order

✅ Increases the chances of achieving the outcome

3.Documents

Can you share information in advance?

Add attachments that can provide some context for the participants.


✅ Saves time on presentations or introductions

✅ Makes meetings more productive

Optional

4.Actions

Should participants bring ideas?

Ask participants to bring suggestions or ideas for the matter to be discussed.


✅ Involves participants actively

✅ Helps avoid group biases
